# Privacy Policy for Floatplane.com Chat Moderator

**Effective Date:** February 28, 2026

## Overview
The Floatplane.com Chat Moderator is a Chrome extension designed to help users moderate and manage chat messages on Floatplane.com by filtering blocked users and staff messages.

## Data Collection and Usage

### Local Storage Only
This extension **does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data** on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API, including:
- **Block list:** Usernames of blocked users
- **Staff filter setting:** Your preference to only show messages from Staff/Moderators

### No External Communication
This extension does **not**:
- Send data to external servers
- Track your activity
- Collect browsing history
- Access your personal information
- Use cookies or tracking mechanisms

### Permissions
This extension requests access to Floatplane.com solely to:
- Read chat messages for filtering purposes
- Hide messages based on the blocked user list or staff message preference

## User Control
You have full control over all data:
- Your block list is stored only on your device (browser storage)
- You can clear your data anytime by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser storage
- Settings are managed entirely within the extension

## Contact
For privacy concerns, please contact the extension developer

## Changes to This Policy
I may update this policy. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of changes
